About the Event

The University’s Bontoc campus commenced its 2025 Intramurals Sports Fest with a bang today, 25 September 2025, as students and personnel colored the streets of Bontoc with a parade and an opening ceremony held at the Eyeb Covered Gym. Originally slated for 22-24 September 2025, it was rescheduled due to Super Typhoon Nando. Bearing the theme “Brewing Strength, Building Unity: Sports and Culture in Action,” six competing teams, namely, Crimson Knights (College of International Hospitality and Sustainable Tourism Management), Blazing Phoenix (College of Accountancy, Business Management and Information Technology), White Pythons (College of Healthcare Education), Green Hornets (College of Criminal Justice Education and Liberal Arts), Blue Wolves (College of Teacher Education) and Wild Falcons (College of Advanced Education) participated in this year’s intramurals. Indigenous and sports games were in full swing after chants and screams filled the opening ceremonies.

In his inspirational message delivered by VP for Internationalization, Planning and Information Management Atty. Dexter C. Lingbanan, University President Dr. Edgar G. Cue challenged the participants to prove that the event is “more than a contest, but a celebration of discipline, camaraderie, and the indomitable spirit of youth.” “Talent winds games, but teamwork and character win championships,” he added. The intramurals will run until Saturday, 27 September 2025.

2025 Intramurals

BLUE WOLVES

The Blue Wolves embody strength, unity, and relentless spirit. Like a wolf pack, we move together — every player supports the next, every effort builds the team. The color blue represents loyalty, determination, and calm under pressure. Whether on offense or defense, we stand our ground and fight with heart. Fierce, focused, and fearless — we are the Blue Wolves.

WILD FALCONS

The Wild Falcons soar high with courage and speed. We strike fast, think smart, and never back down. With our wings spread wide, we dominate the skies — fearless, focused, and free-spirited.

CRIMSON KNIGHTS

The Crimson Knights fight with honor, courage, and discipline. Clad in red, we stand as defenders of victory — strong, bold, and unyielding in the face of any challenge.

BLAZING PHOENIX

Rising from the ashes, the Blazing Phoenix represents rebirth, resilience, and fire. Every loss fuels our next victory — we rise, stronger and brighter than before.

WHITE PYTHONS

The White Pythons are known for their strength, discipline, and precision. Like their namesake, they strike with calculated moves and unstoppable energy. Their teamwork and resilience make them a powerful force on the court and field. Dressed in white, they symbolize purity, unity, and determination, ready to take on any challenge that comes their way.

GREEN HORNETS

The Green Hornets bring speed, agility, and fierce competitiveness to every game. Just like real hornets, they move with purpose—swift, strategic, and always ready to sting the competition. Clad in vibrant green, they represent growth, energy, and teamwork, embodying the spirit of sportsmanship and relentless drive to win.
